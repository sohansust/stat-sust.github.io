Summary of Qualifications

 

Obtained Ph.D in Statistics (in the area of Demography) from Banaras Hindu University, India and M.Phil degree in Statistics (in the area of Bio-statistics) from Rajshahi University, Bangladesh. Have been engaged in research/teaching for more than 16 years. Provided consultancy to various issues including multiple indicator based surveys both in rural and urban areas. Designed and implemented study projects on various issues in micro-level. A good number of research articles published in various reputed journals of home and abroad. Taught Bio-statistics, Statistical Inference and Sampling technique in graduate and under-graduate level courses. Competent in using most of the statistical software used for programming and data management/analysis. Interested areas of research are impact studies on various demographic issues like migration & RCH and developmental issues on aquaculture and agriculture.

 Worked as ‘Consultant – Report Writing’ of “Food Security for Vulnerable Group Development (FSVGD)” Project. This project is funded by European Commission and executed by World Food Programme (WFP) with the co-operation of Ministry of Women and Children Affairs of Bangladesh. The main responsibility of the assignment is to write a report for the impact assessment of the project by analyzing and comparing the impact survey data and baseline data.

 Worked as Project Director of “Sustainable Birth Registration System in Bangladesh – A Case Study in Sunamganj Pourashava”, a Research Project sponsored by Social Science Research Council, Ministry of Planning, Government of Bangladesh.  

 Worked as Consultant for “KAP Survey on Nutrition in the Chittagong Hill Tracts” of Integrated Community Development Project, Chittagong Hill Tracts Development Board, Sponsored by UNICEF.

 Worked as Consultant for “Integrated Baseline Study” of Patuakhali Barguna Aquaculture Extension Project (PBAEP). In this assignment, the key indicators for impact assessment of the project have been identified by extensive analysis of baseline data and prepared the baseline report.

 Worked as Consultant for conducting Multiple Indicator Surveys funded by UNICEF during 1994-1996 both in rural and urban areas in the Chittagong and Sylhet division. The major responsibilities during these assignments were selection and employment of interviewers and supervisors, conducting training of the interviewers, coordination of data collection activities, compilation & analysis of data and preparing reports.

 Worked as the Project Director of "Economic and Demographic Impact of Internal Migration in Bangladesh - A Case Study of Sylhet District" sponsored by University Grants Commission of Bangladesh during 1994 to 1996.

 Worked as the Principal Investigator of  “Impact of Rural Out-Migration on Fertility: A Case Study of Comilla District”, sponsored (technical) by Bangladesh Bureau of Statistics in 1997.

 In Ph.D research (during the appointment as Junior/Senior Research Fellow of UGC of India), I worked on Some Demographic Models and their Applications with Special Reference to Bangladesh. Specifically, I have formulated and/or utilized some mathematical models on migration, age at marriage, marriage migration, and infant and child mortality. These models have also been tested with the data from rural areas of Bangladesh. Moreover, factors influencing rural out-migration, early age at marriage and mortality & child health have also been investigated in this study.
