Message from Head of The Department

                                                                     

 

      The Department of Statistics is proud to be a part of the Shahjalal University of Science and Technology. We invite you to join us in this exciting program of research and practice. The department has an excellent track record in both teaching and research. We offer flexible educational pathways, emphasizing the theoretical, practical, or computational aspects of statistics.

      The existing graduate program of the department serves the needs of future statisticians in science, industry, business, and government, as well as provides the necessary background and stimulation for graduate study. Our graduate program in Statistics emphasizes both theoretical and applied statistics and includes mathematical statistics, probability theory, computing, data analysis, scientific applications, methodology and epidemiology, as well as demography.

     We give equal importance in both research and teaching. Project works are also encouraged to develop innovative and analytical approach to statistics learning. The Department provided all facilities for research work. As a result, many students and teachers enrolled themselves for research degrees in the Department.

      In addition, the department actively promotes postgraduate research and many students go onto study for M.Sc. MPhil, and PhD after the graduation degree.

      The Department is informal and friendly, and the Parishankhan Paribar (an organization of students and teachers) offers a wide variety of cultural and recreational activities.

 

Professor Dr. Md Kabir Hossain
Dean, School of Physical Sciences
Head, Department of Statistics
