Facility

 

→Research and technical supports:

        The department facilitated with various research and technical support. Computer is the most important resource for statisticians. We have two computer lab with sufficient computer and technical support as well as online connection with every computer. One lab is for post graduate students and other is for undergraduate students. An extensive collection of statistical software is also available. The students get every kind of support from this lab.  

 

    
   

 → Seminar Library:

             The department library has a good collection of statistical text books and national & international journals. Seminar staffs are always willing to help with general inquires and other assistance. Monthly research seminars are held in the  department.
