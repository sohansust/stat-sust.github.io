      The Department of Statistics is proud to be a part of the Shahjalal University of Science and Technology. We invite you to join us in this exciting program of research and practice. The department has an excellent track record in both teaching and research. We offer flexible educational pathways, emphasizing the theoretical, practical, or computational aspects of statistics.

     The work of the Department covers a wide range of topics in statistics, both mathematical and theoretical aspects as well as applications to many different fields. The research expertise of the faculty member is reflected in the quality of postgraduate teaching and student supervision, as well as in the wide choice of topics on offer to students for their master's dissertations or research thesis.

     The Statistics department offer MPhil and PhD degree as well as M.Sc degree.
