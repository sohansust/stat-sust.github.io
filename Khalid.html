Profile

Khalidur Rahman (date of Birth-22 December 1976) holds a M.Sc in  Statistics from SUST, 1999. Since February 2004,he is a faculty member of  Statistics at  the department of Statistics. SUST. He had also served in  M.C    Academy as     well as part time teacher in Metropolitan University, Sylhet. He is a life member   of the  Bangladesh Statistical Association (BSA).
