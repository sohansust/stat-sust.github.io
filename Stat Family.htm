What is Stat Family?

Stat family (Parishankhan Paribar) is an association of students and teachers in the department. The cultural, sports and other activities like freshers' reception, farewell, iftar party etc that are performed in the department are organized by the members of this association.

Members:

All the students and the teachers of the department are the members of this association. Each 1st and 2nd boy/girl of a semester represent his semester to the association.

Activities:

Under the banner of stat family we organize various cultural as well as sports program. Stat family also organize picnic and freshers' reception, farewell etc in every semester. Iftar party and get together are also arranged by this family.

Please click on  album to see the pictures of various program that are arranged by the stat family.
