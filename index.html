Department of Statistics

 Shahjalal University of Science And Technology ,Sylhet, Bangladesh

           

        The Shahjalal University of Science and Technology is the first science and  technology university in Bangladesh and many of its departments are at the international forefront for research in various fields. The Department of Statistics has no exception.

       The department offers a full range of graduate and postgraduate courses and also offers postgraduate programs. The research work of the department covers a wide range of topics in statistics, both mathematical and theoretical aspects as well as applications to different fields. The research expertise of the faculties  reflects on the quality of teaching and supervising the students, as well as on the wide choice of topics offered to students.

    

                The scholar-teachers of the Department encourage research activities among the teachers and students. It provides all facilities for research work.The department has two research laboratories and one seminar library with sufficient books. As a result, many students and teachers enrolled themselves for research work in the Department. It also arranges seminars regularly, where the researchers present their papers.

     The department has a association, named Parishankhan Paribar. Various activities like, cultural, sports and picnic, etc are organized by this association. 

 

Note: All information included in the website are given by the corresponding members of the department.
