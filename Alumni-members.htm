Statistics Alumni Association


    Department of Statistics provides a forum for the community of graduate and alumni students. The department offers many opportunities to build your resume while in graduate school. The alumni association involvement is a great way to explore field placement opportunities and network with future employers. You can also connect with other graduate students and alumni of the department.

jamal-sta@sust.edu

Information to be send

Personal detail (including phone number and e-mail address)

Session of study in the department

Permanent address

Current job place and position
